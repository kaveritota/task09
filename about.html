about this page

AJIO is an Indian online fashion and lifestyle brand that offers a wide range of products, including clothing, home decor, beauty products, and electronics: Philosophy: AJIO's philosophy is inclusivity and acceptance. Products: AJIO offers a wide variety of products, including clothing, home decor, beauty products, and electronics. Brands: AJIO offers a wide range of brands, including exclusive international brands, owned labels, and homegrown brands. Initiatives: AJIO offers initiatives such as capsule collections, an indie collection, and AJIO Own. Headquarters: AJIO is headquartered in Bangalore, India. Parent company: AJIO is a digital commerce initiative of Reliance Retail.
